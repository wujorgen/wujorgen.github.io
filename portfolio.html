MArs Cargo ROver (MACRO) Purdue University, ENGR 16100 a.k.a. Project Karma

Video of final demonstration (YouTube) Video of a sensor test run (YouTube)

  • Objective: Autonomously follow a line, traverse obstacles, carry and deposit cargo
  • Processor: RaspberryPi and GrovePi
  • Drivetrain: 2x EV3 Large Motor w/ 3:1 gear reduction
  • Sensors:
    • 2x Line Finder
    • 2x Hall Effect Sensor
    • 1x EV3 Ultrasonic Sensor
  • Performance: Only team to successfully complete 2/3 trials, with partial completion of third and final trial

Taskbot for PA-Robotics event, Technology Student Association, PA Chapter



  • Objective: Traverse a maze, climb obstacles, clear "rubble", and interact with enviromental hazards
  • Processor: VEX ARM Cortex Microcontroller
  • Controller: VEXnet Wireless Joystick
  • Drivetrain: 2x VEX 363 Motor & Treads
  • Manipulators: Waldoes and lift arm are operated by 4x 393 VEX Motor
  • Awards: 1st place, PA-Robotics, PA-TSA State Conference 2018
